## 題目定稿

### Q1｜你截圖最常是要給誰看？（複選）
- 自己（記錄、備忘、學習）
- 同事 / 團隊（工作回報、協作）
- 客戶 / 主管（提案、說明）
- 社群 / 觀眾（內容、教學）

**English — Q1 | Who do you most often take screenshots for? (multi-select)**
- Myself (notes, references, personal learning)
- Colleagues / team (work updates, collaboration)
- Clients / managers (presentations, walkthroughs)
- Community / audience (content creation, tutorials)

---

### Q2｜截完圖之後，您通常還需要再開什麼 App 才能完成工作？（開放式）
說明文字：例如：去 Preview 畫框框、去 Photoshop 打馬賽克、去 Slack 傳圖，請闡述在哪個 App ＋做了什麼事

**English — Q2 | After taking a screenshot, what other apps do you usually need to open to finish the job? (open-ended)**
Helper text: e.g. Preview to draw boxes, Photoshop to blur sensitive areas, Slack to send the image — please describe which app + what you did
